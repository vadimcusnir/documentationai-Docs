---
title: Demo Mode Not Working
description: Fix demo mode access problems in CUSNIR OS.
---

## Symptoms

- Demo page does not load
- Features appear disabled

## Common Causes

- Browser cookies disabled
- Outdated browser version

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable cookies">
    Ensure cookies are enabled in your browser settings for `cusniros.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your browser">
    Use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Edge for best compatibility.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
If issues persist, try clearing your browser cache or switching devices.
</Callout>
