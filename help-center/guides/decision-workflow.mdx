## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Demo Mode">
    Go to `https://cusniros.com/demo` and sign in if you have an account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Governance Panel">
    Click the **Governance** tab on the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate New Workflow">
    Select **New Workflow** and choose a decision template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Submit">
    Enter details, review steps, and click **Start**. In demo, results are simulated.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
For real decisions, register an account and exit demo mode.
</Callout>
